## Set Up Your Email Campaign

Kick off your email marketing journey by setting up your campaign and planning your strategy.

### Create and Preview Your Email

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="github" title="Clone your docs locally">
    During the onboarding process, we created a repository on your Github with
    your docs content. You can find this repository on our
    [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com). To clone the repository
    locally, follow these
    [instructions](https://docs.github.com/en/repositories/creating-and-managing-repositories/cloning-a-repository)
    in your terminal.
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Design Your Email Template">
    Start by selecting a professional email template that reflects your brand. Customize layouts, colors, and fonts using our intuitive editor to create a visually appealing message.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="eye" title="Preview Your Email">
    Ensure your email looks perfect on all devices. Use our live preview mode to see how your email renders on both desktop and mobile, making adjustments as needed.
    <Note>
      Testing across different devices is key to maximizing engagement. Verify that your images, links, and formatting display correctly before sending.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Send Your Campaign

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Schedule Your Send">
    Optimize your campaign by scheduling the send time based on your audience’s time zones and engagement patterns. Set it and forget it—our system will handle the rest.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="paper-plane" title="Launch Your Email">
    Once you’re satisfied with your preview, hit send. Monitor real-time analytics to track open rates, click-throughs, and overall campaign performance immediately after launch.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
